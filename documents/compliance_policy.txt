Compliance Policy

Compliance teams must ensure that banking operations follow internal policies and regulatory requirements.
Any unusual account activity should be reviewed carefully.
Access to confidential financial documents should be controlled using role-based access.
Policy violations must be reported to the compliance team.
Employees should follow data governance, access control, and audit requirements when handling sensitive financial data.
Wire Transfer Policy

International wire transfers must be reviewed before processing. High-risk wire transfers require additional verification, customer authentication, and manager approval. Suspicious wire transfer activity must be reported to the fraud and compliance teams.
